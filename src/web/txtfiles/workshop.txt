<P><h2>3-Day Problem Characterization and Risk Remediation Analysis
Training -- Featuring the FRAMES, MEPAS<SUP>®</SUP>, and SUM<SUP>3</SUP>
Software packages with other related tools.</h2>
<P><B>I</B>n this course you will learn concepts and methodologies for
evaluating health impacts and remediation alternatives for environmental
problems and issues. The sessions begin with defining the problem and
characterizing the site. With this information you will then convert the problem
into a conceptual site model. You will analyze both health/environmental impacts
and remediation alternatives that will be applied to evaluate the environmental
problem.
<P>You will integrate and evaluate constituent source, fate/transport through
environmental media for multiple exposure routes to achieve health consequences
for radionuclide and chemicals, using the MEPAS<SUP>®</SUP> software package.
<UL>Other topics that you will address are:
  <LI>System Application
  <LI>Sensitivity and Uncertainty Analysis
  <LI>Exposure Parameters
  <LI>Benchmarking
  <LI>Modular Risk Approach
  <LI>Calibration
