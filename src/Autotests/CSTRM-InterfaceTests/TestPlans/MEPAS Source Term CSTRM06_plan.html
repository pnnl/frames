Automated Test for CSTRM06

PURPOSE

This case verifies that the CSTRM correctly calculates an infiltration rate based on varying monthly climatological and site data. Climatology data was entered that described a site that on average had all wet months. The constituent Chromium VI was selected for this case.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Compute time-varying water flow rates (infiltration and overland runoff) based on site characterization and meteorological (monthly climatology) data.
